Welcome to the DFV scoring pages!

These are the official result pages of the Ultimate Frisbee department of the German Discsport Association (Deutscher Frisbeesportverband e. V.). Please select the appropriate season and tournament pages in the navigation on the left.

  • You can find detailed information about modes, leagues, promotion and relegation on the pages of the event format committee (Spielordnungskomitee).
  • For more general information about Ultimate in Germany, please visit the pages of the Ultimate section and the DFV.
